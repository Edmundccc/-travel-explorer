    Stay Safe

    The five things we always check.

    General notes — always cross-check with your country's travel advisory.

    General Precautions

    • Photo-scan your passport, insurance, and vaccination card — store in cloud + offline.
    • Carry a backup payment card on a separate person/bag.
    • Share your daily plan with someone back home.
    • Memorise your hotel address in the local language.

    Scams to Avoid

    • "Closed temple" detours that funnel you into a gem shop.
    • Unmetered taxis at airports — book the official counter or app.
    • Free bracelets, friendship strings, or "tasting" plates at restaurants.
    • ATM skimmers — prefer machines inside bank branches.

    Emergency Numbers

    • Singapore — 999 police, 995 ambulance/fire
    • Japan — 110 police, 119 ambulance/fire
    • Thailand — 191 police, 1669 medical, 1155 tourist police
    • Indonesia — 110 police, 118 ambulance
    • Australia — 000 all emergencies

    Health & Vaccinations

    • Check Hep A, Typhoid, Japanese Encephalitis for SEA trips.
    • Pack rehydration salts, paracetamol, antihistamine, and plasters.
    • Drink bottled or filtered water in BKK / Bali / Hanoi.
    • Travel insurance with medevac is non-negotiable.

    Solo Travel Tips

    • Arrive in daylight wherever possible.
    • Book the first two nights — improvise later.
    • Choose well-lit, central neighbourhoods over cheaper outskirts.
    • Trust your gut. A skipped activity is cheaper than a bad night.
